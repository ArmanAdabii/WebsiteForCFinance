Company Description

Petrobras (Petróleo Brasileiro S.A.) is a state-controlled Brazilian multinational corporation in the petroleum industry. As one of the largest producers of oil and gas in the world, it plays a pivotal role in Brazil's economy and the global energy market.

Project Summary

This project evaluates the financial health, capital structure, and intrinsic value of Petrobras. Utilizing data from fiscal years 2021–2025, we analyze how the company navigates volatile oil prices and political shifts in the South American landscape.

Main Results

  • Intrinsic Value: Based on my DCF analysis, the estimated value is significantly different from the current market price.
  • Financial Health: Strong cash flow generation despite fluctuations in the cost of capital.
  • Key Takeaway: High dividend yields remain a core attraction for investors, though regulatory risks persist.
