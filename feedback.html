Midterm Presentation Comments

The following feedback was provided during the midterm review phase, which directly informed the final structure, data inclusion, and narrative of this website:

Overall Feedback

The analysis is pretty good. It is great that you added graphs of certain financial ratios, but the main problem is that you omitted lots of important information.

  1. There is no indication of the earnings and revenues generated by the business segments.
  2. There is no table with all the financial ratios. How can I make sense of the working capital ratios, operating returns, and liquidity ratios?
  3. The cost of capital is missing the effective tax rate, which you need to add. Another thing not clear to me is why you claim that alpha is above market expectations. You need to be clearer about this and provide more information if you intend to discuss this issue.
  4. The DDM and DFCF need tables where you show all the information, as I did. For example, in the DDM, there is no indication of the estimated price, which is problematic.
  5. Judging from your preliminary results, there is mixed evidence on whether the company is overvalued or undervalued. You need to look at what analysts' estimates say to understand more about this. What is the geopolitical risk that the company is subject to? Why does it explain 50% of the discount?
